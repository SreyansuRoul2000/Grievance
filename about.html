About

The purpose of this community is to foster a spirit of loyalty and to promote the general welfare of our Institution. This Alumni Association exist to support Trident’s goals, and to strengthen the ties between alumni, the community, and the Institution . Today, alumni relations is an important part of an institution's advancement activities for many reasons: Alumni are an institution's most loyal supporters. Alumni are fundraising prospects. They take their knowledge of the institution to their hometowns and countries and into their professional and social networks. Alumni members are always in the loop. Career opportunities and networking events are two of the biggest reasons to join. Alumni support is essential for keeping this pride alive.
